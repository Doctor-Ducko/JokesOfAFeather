# Jokes Of A Feather || Doctor Ducko

## A Balatro Mod adding a whole buncha stuffs

# Overview
Jokes of a Feather adds new Jokers, Decks, Enhancements, Seals, and a whole new Consumable type (Trinkets) to Balatro!

Currently added:
* 45 Jokers
    * 12 Common
    * 16 Uncommon
    * 8 Rare
    * 3 Legendary
    * 6 Family Guy
* 4 Decks
* 5 Tarot Cards
* 5 Spectral Cards
* 12 Trinket Cards
* 2 Vouchers
* 5 Enhancements
* 4 Seals
* 4 Booster Packs
